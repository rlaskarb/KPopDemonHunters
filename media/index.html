Heroes clash with demons in disguise.

HUNTR/X

Rumi, Mira, and Joy—the global K-pop trio who secretly fight as demon hunters. Guided by Rumi’s voice, Mira’s fierce moves, and Joy’s lyrical fire, they use songs as Soul Gates to seal demons. Behind their stardom lies a sacred duty as the last line of defense for humanity.

SAJA BOYS

Jinu, Abby, Romance, Baby, and Mystery—the flawless five who conquered the world. Named after death messengers, they charm fans with talent and allure, but hide their true nature as demons harvesting souls. Idol façades mask their mission, making them HUNTR/X’s deadliest rivals.

DUFFY

The blue tiger spirit and Jinwoo’s loyal companion. Intimidating at first with glowing eyes, yet clumsy and lovable—always trying to fix fallen things, always failing with charm. A harmless guardian who can slip through dimensions.

SHOCKING: Lumi Quits? The Truth Behind Her Sudden Disappearance

Panic spread among fans when Lumi was noticeably absent from Joy and Mira's cozy movie night setup, sparking wild speculation about potential group drama or departure rumors across social media platforms. However, the mystery was quickly solved when their manager's Instagram revealed the innocent truth: Lumi had simply gone to bed early after their exhausting concert performance, while Joy and Mira prepared for a well-deserved movie marathon during their official two-week rest period.
